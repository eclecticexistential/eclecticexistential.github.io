Basic Simulation and Stats

As a fan of MTG, I wanted to simulate the odds of one deck winning over another.

To replicate this on a basic level, I made a program that creates two decks based on input, such as number of lands, types of spells, and creatures, then ran the simulation 100,000 times.

  • Decks: Evolving Wilds vs Non-Evolving Wilds
  • Creatures: 1 or 5 points
  • Spells: Tutor, Draw, Gain Life, Lightning Bolt, and Removal
  • High Roll Goes First
  • Draw or Play
  • Summoning Sickness
